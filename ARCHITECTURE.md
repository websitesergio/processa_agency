# ARCHITECTURE.md - Elite Consultancy Standard

## 1. THE "ANTI-NERD" DESIGN LAWS
- **Massive Whitespace:** Every section MUST have `py-32` or `py-40`. The site must feel empty, elegant, and breathable. No cramped boxes.
- **Huge Typography:**
  - Main Headlines: `text-5xl` or `text-7xl`, Serif font (Playfair Display), perfectly centered.
  - Body text: `text-lg` or `text-xl`, `leading-relaxed`, max-width `max-w-2xl` for perfect readability.
- **Zero Tech UI:** NO grid backgrounds. NO terminal windows. NO blinking cursors. NO complex flowcharts. NO status dots.
- **Colors:** Background is `bg-[#0a0a0a]`. Text is pure white `text-white` for headers and `text-zinc-400` for body. Accent is elegant gold `text-amber-500` for the calculated revenue numbers.

## 2. THE JARGON PURGE (Copy Requirements)
You are strictly forbidden from using the following words: AEO, Nodes, Terminal, Latency, Vectors, AI, Routing, Architecture, Ledger, Retrieval.

**Use these replacements globally:**
- "AEO / SearchGPT" → "Digital Discovery"
- "Intent Capture Architecture" → "Patient Acquisition System"
- "Automated Patient Routing" → "60-Second Lead Response"
- "Terminal Triage" → "The Processa Engine"
