Answer Engine Optimisation for Dental Practices

In 2026, the majority of high-intent dental patient journeys begin with a query to an AI search engine rather than traditional keyword search. A patient asks ChatGPT, "What is the best dental implant clinic in London?" or Perplexity, "Where should I get Invisalign in Europe?" These queries produce a single cited answer, not a list of ten options. If your practice is cited as that answer, the patient learns about your clinic at the moment they are most engaged in research. If your practice is not cited, a competitor appears in that position instead. Being the cited answer in AI search is not the same as ranking on page one of Google in traditional search. It is a competition for entity authority, not keyword position. Processa builds the semantic schema markup, entity verification signals, and authoritative content architecture that positions your practice for citation across every major AI platform operating in European markets.

Answer Engine Optimisation compounds over 60 to 90 days as AI systems index and verify your clinic's new content architecture. For practices investing in Google Ads or Meta advertising for implant and Invisalign patient acquisition, establishing organic citation authority through AEO provides a growing complement to paid channels. Patients cited your practice through AI search carry zero advertising cost and typically convert at higher rates than cold paid traffic because the AI citation carries implicit endorsement. The combination of AEO citation authority and paid advertising amplification produces the highest-efficiency patient acquisition environment in modern dental marketing.
