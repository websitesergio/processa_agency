In 2026, the majority of high-intent dental implant patient journeys begin with a conversational query to an AI search engine rather than a traditional keyword search. A patient asks ChatGPT, "What is the best dental implant clinic in London?" or Perplexity AI, "Where should I get implants in Spain?" or Google AI Overviews, "Dental implant cost and timeline." These queries are not heading to a list of ten blue links. They are going to a single cited answer — one clinic that the AI system has identified as the most authoritative, trusted source for that specific query.

Being cited in AI search results is not the same as ranking on page one of Google in traditional search. Traditional search is a competition for keyword position. AI search is a competition for entity authority. The AI system is asking, "Which dental clinic should I cite as the authoritative answer to this question?"
